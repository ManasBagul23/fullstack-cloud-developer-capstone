Our Mission

We believe that buying a car should be a transparent and enjoyable experience. Our platform connects car buyers with trusted dealerships and provides honest reviews from real customers.

Founded in 2020, Dealership Review has grown to become the most trusted source for dealer reviews across the United States. We use advanced sentiment analysis technology to help you understand customer experiences at a glance.

  • ✅ AI-powered sentiment analysis
  • ✅ Comprehensive dealer information
  • ✅ Easy-to-use search and filter
